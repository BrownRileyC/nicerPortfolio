About Me

My name is Riley Brown. I was born and raised in the suburbs of Philadelphia. I attended William Penn Charter school and then studied psychology at the University of Vermont with a focus on developmental psychology. It's a fascinating field, in my opinion, and something very deserving of more study.

After graduating I decided to pursue a career working with young children, but not as a counselor and instead an educator. I was lucky to find a position working as a preschool and kindergarten prepratory teacher.

After my two years working with these children I realized that while I love working with kids that young, it wasn't what I wanted to do the rest of my life. That's what led me here.
